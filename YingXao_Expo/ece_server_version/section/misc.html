We consumed a lot of Xinjiang Big Plate Chicken during work sessions... and I mean A LOT.

By Allen Chen

I mean, hey, who doesn't love chicken.

Especially after we have been channeling your focus at a highly sophisticated system for hours, we feel extra hungry and crave for some good food. That's when Xinjiang Big Plate Chicken, also known as Dapanji, comes to rescue. It's a classic northwestern Chinese dish where we throw chicken, onions, peppers, potatoes and thick noodles in to a big spicy stew.

It almost became a tridition for us to gather on Saturdays, order a Dapanji and work through the day with the fragrance of chicken free-flowing in the apartment. Whether that boosts our productivity remains an unanswered question. What was clear, however, was that, boy it smells good.

Oh, and it tastes good too. (Of course!)
