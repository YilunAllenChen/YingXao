Why Vanilla?

By Allen Chen

Why use plain old Bootstrap + HTML5 + jQuery, instead of any other powerful frameworks like React.js or Vue.js ?

The answer is simple: We want to be simple.

Though modern frontend frameworks provide declaritive and object-oriented-programming-like syntaxes and hence support all kinds of fancy functionalities and are easy to scale, they do have a rather steep learning curve, and tend to take longer to setup, build and maintain with a small team (as small a 1 person).

On the other hand, the old-schooled tech stack that we are using here is a quick-starter. It's really easy to setup pages, have some basic interative functionalities and quite easy to maintain. We don't worry about callback functinos, states of the components, etc., but instead, we focus only on how things look like, where they should be, and in rare occasions, if they link to the correct sites.
